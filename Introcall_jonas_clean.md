- **What Platinion is:** BCG's tech arm — senior, architecture-led. Delivers strategy, roadmaps and architecture with small teams, *not* armies of developers. Sized like BCG (~20k globally); Platinion much smaller.

- **AI positioning:** Not a siloed track — everyone needs enterprise-AI training; specializations (AI / Cybersecurity / ERP) sit on top of a generalist expectation, with identical pay/progression.
- **Partnerships:** Senior-to-senior, less standardized and more personal/interactive than at a Big Four (which he found more process-heavy).
- **Zürich:** Platinion ~12 people (BCG CH ~200+), growing, not yet in Geneva; Wealth Management focus drives some London work. Actively hiring (hence the headhunter).
- **Process (next steps):** MBB-style — 4 interviews in 2 blocks of 2 (~2.5h each; first longer, second shorter). Cases may be architecture-specific. Final interview is with a Director and covers salary + joining terms. Fit (methodical + personal) is what matters most.
